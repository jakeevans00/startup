About Us

TrainClimbing.com is managed by a group of passionate climbers based out of Utah. The site is intended as a launching point for climbers who have been actively training for at least a year. Many of the workouts are strenous and can damage tendons if adequate precautions are not taken. Be sure to follow us on social media and, above all, no half sends!
